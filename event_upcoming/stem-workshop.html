November 25, 2024

STEM Youth Workshop: AI & Robotics

Hands-on workshop for youth aged 12-18 featuring AI programming, robotics projects, and 3D printing demonstrations

November 25, 2024
10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
EvoAFuture Learning Center
Ages 12-18
FREE EVENT

Limited spots available - First come, first served

Workshop Overview

Join us for an exciting full-day STEM workshop designed to introduce young minds to the fascinating world of artificial intelligence and robotics! This hands-on workshop provides participants with direct experience in programming, AI concepts, robotics engineering, and 3D printing technology.

What You'll Learn

AI Programming Basics

Introduction to machine learning concepts using Python and visual programming tools. Create your first AI chatbot!

Robotics Engineering

Build and program autonomous robots using Arduino kits. Learn sensors, motors, and basic autonomous navigation.

3D Design & Printing

Design custom robot parts using CAD software and watch them come to life with our 3D printers.
